About Me

Designing meaningful

digital experiences

With around five years of experience in the IT industry, I have built digital products across mobile apps, web platforms, dashboards, and brand experiences, with most of my journey centered around UX/UI design. I focus on creating intuitive, visually refined experiences that align user needs with business goals.

My work spans healthcare, education, wellness, creator economy, and social platforms, giving me the ability to adapt across domains and solve complex problems through clear design thinking. I also leverage AI assisted development workflows to rapidly turn ideas into high quality digital products.

Quick

Insights

More about my approach, decisions, and design thinking.

What makes you a strong product designer?

I combine user centric thinking with structured problem solving to design solutions that are intuitive, scalable, and aligned with business goals.

How do you approach a new design problem?

I start by understanding users and context, define the problem clearly, explore solutions, and iterate based on feedback and insights.

How do you handle feedback and iterations?

I treat feedback as a key part of the process, refining designs continuously to improve usability and overall experience.

How do you ensure consistency in design?

By building design systems and maintaining consistency across components, interactions, and visual patterns.

How do you ensure smooth design handoff?

I ensure smooth handoff by providing clear specifications, organized assets, and staying closely aligned with developers during implementation.
